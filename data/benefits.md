# OnePass Select — NJ Membership Benefits Knowledge Base
Source: OnePass Select NJ Tier Participation Guide

NOTE:
Gym availability varies by exact location, franchise ownership, and employer plan configuration.
Tier placement is representative and must be confirmed in the OnePass portal for a specific NJ ZIP code.

---

## Tier Mapping (Internal System)

Base Tier     → OnePass Classic  
Bronze Tier   → OnePass Standard  
Silver Tier   → OnePass Premium  
Gold Tier     → OnePass Elite  

---

## BASE TIER (Classic Equivalent)

### Typical Facility Access
- Planet Fitness (NJ locations; Classic-level access)
- Retro Fitness (many NJ locations)
- Blink Fitness (single-club access)
- LA Fitness (franchise/location dependent)
- New York Sports Club (NYSC) (many NJ clubs)
- 24 Hour Fitness (limited NJ presence)

### Member Benefits
- Access to big-box commercial gym equipment
- Locker room facilities
- Cardio equipment
- Strength training machines
- Standard free-weight areas
- Basic floor access during staffed hours

### Usage Notes
- Membership type may vary by location (e.g., PF Classic vs Black Card)
- Multi-club access may not be included

## BRONZE TIER (Standard Equivalent)

### Studio Access (Limited Monthly Sessions)
- CycleBar
- Row House
- Barre3
- YogaSix
- AKT
- P.volve

### Member Benefits
- Access to boutique studio classes (capped sessions/month)
- Cycling classes
- Barre / yoga training sessions

### Usage Notes
- Class/session caps apply

---

## Tier Eligibility Guidance

- Big-box gyms (Planet Fitness, Retro, NYSC) are typically accessible at Base Tier.
- Cycle, yoga, and barre studios generally require Bronze or Silver Tier.
- Premium studios and luxury gym networks (Life Time, Orangetheory, F45) typically require Gold Tier.

## Location Verification
